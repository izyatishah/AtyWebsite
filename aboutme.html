Welcome!

I'm Nur Izyati Binti Shah Nizam, a 20-year-old Malaysian, born on June 1, 2004, and living in Rembau, Negeri Sembilan. Being Malay is a big part of who I am, and I enjoy connecting with both my culture and modern trends. One of my favorite hobbies is shopping, which lets me explore new styles and find things that really suit my personality. I love the thrill of finding unique pieces that feel like they were made just for me. For me, shopping is more than just buying things—it’s a way to express myself and stay connected with what’s new, balancing my roots and my interests.
